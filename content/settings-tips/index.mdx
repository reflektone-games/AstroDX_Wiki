### Introduction

This page contains a list of tips on how you can improve the feel of the game.

## Portrait Mode

In landscape mode (when your phone is rotated 90 degrees), your phone's notification bar can block your fingers during gameplay.

Try changing the **screen orientation** option in **Settings -> System** to **portrait**.

<Callout type="info">
    You can also use the [Guided Access](https://support.apple.com/111795) mode on iOS or the [App Pinning](https://support.google.com/android/answer/9455138) feature on some Android phones.
</Callout>
